My Journey in MIS 4v98

This semester, I've been working through an intensive course on AI systems and their real-world applications. What started as theory quickly turned into hands-on experimentation—building multi-agent systems, generating creative content with AI, and developing full-stack web applications to showcase it all.

From training AI agents to automate complex workflows, to exploring generative image and video models, to building this Django portfolio—each project pushed my understanding of what's possible with AI. The focus has always been practical: How can these tools actually solve problems? How do you build at scale?

This portfolio is a living record of that semester. It's not just a showcase of finished projects—it's proof of the learning journey. Every agent we built, every model we experimented with, and every line of Django code represents "vibe coding" in action: thoughtful development, deep understanding, and real innovation.
